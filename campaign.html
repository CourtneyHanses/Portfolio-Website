COURTNEY HANSES

04-08/2024

2024 CAMPAIGN

CLIENT: My father, Phil Hanses

Over the summer, my dad had someone running against him in the election primaries for the county. I designed many assets for him, including a palm card, newpaper ads, business cards, and postcards. Since I made so many things, it helped me with creating a cohesive identity with design elements.

It was a bit of a challenge to include everything he requested without overcrowding the page. To achieve this, I played with visual hierarchy by changing the point sizes and font weights. There is a lot of writing, but I made sure to make the more important words larger so the eye has somewhere to go to instead of being swamped by long blocks of text.


Courtney Hanses, 2025
